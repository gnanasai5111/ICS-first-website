About Us

Innovation Communication Systems Ltd. is a technology-driven orgaization with a strong focus on communication product development and software services. Established in year 1988, ICS started its journey under the commendable leadership of a group of technocrats.

Under their perceptive guidance, ICS has witnessed unbelievable growth in the business on an accelerated pace. The farsighted approach and strong business ethics are the main reasons why we are known as one of the well-known security as well as telecom solutions service providers, based in india.

Over the years, the company has spread its wings across the country due to its optimum quality products and reliable services. The major clients of the ICS include leading cellular/basic telecom operators, public sector organizations and defense labs.
