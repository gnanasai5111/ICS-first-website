Four Channel Frame Grabber Card

Home Defence Four Channel Frame Grabber Card

4-Channel Frame Grabber card concurrently captures four channels of Analog NTSC/PAL video and four channels of stereo audio. Base board captures raw video frames from each channel at up to full frame rate—30 frames/s for NTSC and 25 frames/s for PAL. Four BNC connectors on the mounting bracket can be used to connect external composite video sources. Four 3.5mm audio jack connector connects Line-in stereo audio inputs. Two RS422 ports- one for user data and another one for GUI parameters handshaking.
The unit supports VGA, DVI-D display ports; this unit supports NTSC video captures frame rates up to 30 frames per second (fps) at an aspect ratio of 720x480.supports PAL video capture frame rates up to 25 fps and a 720x576 aspect ratio. Audio can be stereo or mono; the sampling rate supports 8 kHz to 96 kHz. The high frame-video capture and stream out either through a single-lane x1 PCIe interface or Gigabit LAN port which makes it suitable for the board for applications such as broadcast-grade video/audio capturing. This unit got one POE Gigabit port to support video surveillance, law enforcement, and traffic monitoring. LED indication for video Capture rate.

Main Features

  • H.264/AVC baseline, main or high profile video compression up to L4.1.
  • Uncompressed raw data sent to the host and H.264 compressed data available from on-board 1Gb Ethernet outputs.
  • 4 × Composite video inputs (NTSC/PAL).
  • 6U size.
  • 4 Stereo Line-In audio inputs.
  • 2-stereo HP-Out Audio headset ports.
  • 1-DVI-I display support.
  • 1-PCIe-1X.
  • 2-GigE PORTS; one supports POE.
  • 4-UART PORTS- 2-RS422,2-RS232, 2-USB 2.0 Ports.
  • 1-TB SSD hard drive.
  • 16GB eMMC NAND FLASH storage.
  • 1-micro-SD card slot.
  • Low Power Consumption (≈10W), Conduction cooled device.
  • GUI based Channel selection for video capture and data store format.

Unit Specifications

  • Mil-std-461E :CE101, CS101, CS114, CS116, RE102, RE103, RS103.
  • Operating Temperature : -40°C to 85°C (Rugged).
  • Non-operating Temperature: -55°C to 105°C (Rugged).
  • Vibration (sine) : 0.1 g2/Hz (15-2K Hz) (Rugged).
  • Vibration (random) : 0.1 g2/Hz (15-2K Hz) (Rugged).
  • Shock : 40g peak, 11ms half-sine (Rugged).
  • Conformal coated.
  • Humidity 95% without condensation.
  • Dimensions (L × W) and Weight 160 × 233 mm x 10.0 oz (Rugged).
  • Software/Platform Support: Full SDK support for Windows or Linux.
