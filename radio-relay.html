High Capacity Radio Relay

Home Defence High Capacity Radio Relay—IRR-B4

The IRR-B4 is a High Capacity Radio Relay (HCRR) operating in Band –IV (4400 MHz to 5000 MHz) capable of Full-Duplex Data transfer up to 100 Mbps.

IRR-B4 can be deployed for

  • 1. Point to Point (P2P) communication link, with data rates 2,8, 34 & 100 Mbps, for a range of 20 km in Fixed Frequency mode.
  • 2. Point to Multi Point (P2MP) Communication Link of over 20km with 8Mbps aggregated data rate for outstations.

  • In addition to P2P and P2MP modes, the system can also be configured to serve as a single hop repeater, with 2, 8, 34 & 100 Mbps data rates, for establishing communication up to 35km.
  • The system has inbuilt ECCM features such as Frequency hopping, Frequency evasion, Adaptive Frequency Control and Adaptive Power Control.
  • In frequency hopping mode, communication over a range of 20km is possible with 8 Mbps Data Rate.
  • The above features of IRR-B4 are also characterized by optimized transmit power and RF channel band width.
  • System has E1, E3 and Ethernet base band interfaces, with both Electrical and Optical connectivity. System accepts 230 V AC and +48 V DC with auto changeover facility.
  • System has Narrow beam antenna for P2P communication and Sector Beam Antenna for P2MP communication.

Configuration & Features

  • Indoor Data Processing Unit (IDPU)
  • Outdoor Radio Unit (ORU)
  • Telescopic Mast
  • Antenna Alignment Unit
  • Antennae (5 deg Grid, 120 deg Sector)
  • Power Supply Units (PSU); Cables and Accessories.
  • 16 hop frequency spots spread over 4400 MHz to 5000 MHz with a hop rate of 1000 Hops/s
  • E1, E3 and Ethernet base band interfaces, with both Electrical and Optical connectivity.
  • Point to Point mode for 20km range for Data Rates of 2, 8, 34, 100 Mbps.
  • GPS for location identification.
  • Point to Multi Point Mode range of 15km with 8 Mbps aggregated data rate.
  • ECCM
  • Modem Modulations: QPSK, 16 QAM, 64 QAM
  • Repeater mode range of 35km in P2P configuration.
  • Power On Self Test (POST).
  • Built In Test Equipment (BITE).
  • Environmental: JSS55555 Std Rev 03.
    • ODU: Class L3
    • IDU : Class L2B
  • EMI/EMC : As per MIL 461E
