Up Down Converter (8 - 12 GHz)

Home Catalogue Up Down Converter (8 - 12 GHz)

The Up Down converter configured in three sections; Up conversion, Down conversion and Control section. The Up Conversion performs the conversion of IF (customized) signal to RF Signal. The Down conversion converts the RF signal to IF signal (customized). Low Noise, high stability Frequency synthesizers are built in. Digital control of signal level in Upconverter, synthesizers and filter selection, user interface and display are controlled/ programmed using a microcontroller based circuit.

Application

  • RADAR Target Echo Simulator

Features

  • X Band Up Down Converters with 1000 MHz Channel Bandwidth.
  • Low Noise Synthesizer.
  • Digital Step Attenuators.
  • Highly selective Band pass Filters.
  • Remote and Local Operation.
  • Parameters and Status display.
