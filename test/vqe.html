The Voice Quality Challenge

Customers increasingly expect better reliability and performance of Voice Quality services during a long distance call from Operators but Operators expand their new applications to more sophisticated manners without offering a standard quality voice to the existing and potential customers. In order to secure existing customers, service providers must verify the availability, reliability and quality of their Network Voice Call service offerings, which means that speech quality, unwanted noise reduction, echo cancellation and other calling features must be continuously tested to ensure they are stable. Under these circumstances, Operators should learn all they can about the factors that impact network quality and how to best manage it. We understand that telecom operators need systems that meet their specific requirements at strategic as well as at operational levels.
